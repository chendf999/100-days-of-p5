About

100 days of p5 is a creative coding challenge by Daphne Chen. The goal is to test and learn basic generative art and visualization with p5.js. Due to the experimental nature of this project, many code sketches created are variants of tutorial exercises, recreations of existing visual effects, or translation of a similar WebGL project. All the code sketches are available on CodePen and Github.

To learn more about the process and takeaways, please go to Daphne’s 100 days of p5.

Have questions or feedback? Please send a message to chdap@outlook.com.
